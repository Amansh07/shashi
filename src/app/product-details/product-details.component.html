Hand Knotted

Hand knotted rugs are made by precisely tying several knots in a carpet. Traditionally, the knotted rugs are made on a designed loom. Vertical cotton threads are tied onto the loom which becomes the fringe of the carpet.

Pet Loom Durry

This durrie, is essentially a cotton and wool mix woven thick floor covering. Besides cotton, jute, rayon and chenille are also used as raw materials to weave different and custom durries as per the requirement. The weaving is carried out manually by skilled artisans on a traditional horizontal loom or vertical loom.
